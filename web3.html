About me

Dr. M. Senthamil Selvi is currently working as Professor & Head in the Department of Information Technology at Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College, Coimbatore. She has 24 years of teaching experience and 3 years of industry experience. She has guided many UG and PG projects. Her research and teaching interests include Wireless Sensor Networks, Mobile Computing, Internet of Things and Social Mobile Analytics Cloud. She has participated & organized various seminars, workshops, and Faculty Development Programmes and also published several papers in Conferences and Journals.
